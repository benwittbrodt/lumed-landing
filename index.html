What's inside

Everything a collector needs

Five focused tools built around how watch enthusiasts actually think about their pieces.

Watch Box

Your full collection in a clean grid. Add photos, reference numbers, case details, and notes. Sort by brand, most worn, or recently added. Favourites always float to the top.

Wear Log

Record which watch you wore each day, with optional occasion notes. Wear multiple watches in one day — Lumed tracks them all.

Stats

See your collection at a glance — most worn pieces, neglected watches, and monthly activity across 30 days, 90 days, or all time. Know which watch needs a wrist day.

Time Sync

A live, NTP-synced clock accurate to the millisecond — so you can set your watch with precision. Add world time zones to set your GMT complication easily.

Accuracy Tracking

Photograph your watch face — Confim the time and Lumed compares it to NTP time to calculate daily drift in seconds. Track whether it's gaining or losing over time and whether or not it's time to re-set.

Movement Specs

Define each watch's movement specs to measure accuracy compared to expectations. COSC, METAS, Rolex Superlative, and other common movement types are default or add custom specs.

Photo-based measurement

Know your drift, down to the second

Outside from a professional timegrapher the best way to track your watch's accuracy is real wear checkpoints. Lumed does all the math and you just need to take a photo, verify the time, and see how well your watch is running. Outputs are relative to your defined specification and Lumed also reminds you to reset the baseline depending on the power reserve of your watch.
